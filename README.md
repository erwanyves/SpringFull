The objective of this macro is to design cylindrical compression springs with FreeCAD.

Available features:
    Calculation based on mean, external, or internal diameter.
    List of standard wire diameters.
    Choice of 14 materials selected from the most commonly used.
    4 available end coil configurations: cut, ground, closed, closed and ground.
    Two persistent LCS (Local Coordinate Systems) for compatibility with assemblies.
    5 representation options:
        Free state.
        Under load.
        Solid height.
        At user-defined height.
        At user-defined load.
    Simplified mode (enclosing tube) to save memory.
    For now, 5 languages, to be checked:
    -en.
    -fr.
    -de.
    -it.
    -es.
